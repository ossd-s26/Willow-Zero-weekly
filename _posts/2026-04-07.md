i dont like the industry very much, and far prefer open models of development.. i think they are more productive and create better software in the long run. i see why large corporations like using open source. its free, they can mooch off of others' work if they really want to, or influence a project toward their particular use case rather than that of the actual average user. i know corporate adoption is generally seen as a good thing, but i c cant help but feel that its turned opensource development into yet another extension of the capitalist workplace.
