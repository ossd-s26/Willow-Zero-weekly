# Open Source! 

I've been interested in open source software for years. Im writing this from my laptop, which is running [Arch Linux](https://archlinux.org/), using [nano](https://www.nano-editor.org/), an open source text editor, inside of [kitty](https://sw.kovidgoyal.net/kitty/), an open source terminal emulator. I make music which I edit in [audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/), I browse the internet using [firefox](https://www.firefox.com), and I communicate with my friends and loved ones using [Signal](https://signal.org). 

All of these are open source projects, and I actively seek to fill my highly digitized life with such projects, as I find that while they can be finnicky, can lack support, and can have eccentric communities, they agree more with my sensibilities than proprietary alternatives. 

Open source stands in opposition to enshittification, to capitalist capture of every moment of our lives. It stands against, at least in principle, the monetization of our eyes, ears, minds, and attention. It stands against AI proliferation, against privatization, and against the death of the commons. Open source projects stand as a testament to what we as humans can do, when we dont allow ourselves to give in to greed and the dog-eat-dog mentality we are taught from the start in American society.
